Review of Mass Effect 3

Mass Effect 3 is the last game in the story of Commander Shepard, released in 2012 developed by BioWare and published by EA. After the events of Mass Effect 2 Shepard has been relieved of duty and stripped of his rank due to his time with Cerberus. He has been kept around as an "advisor" on how to deal with the Reapers. After the games start, Earth has been attacked by the Reapers. Shepard is then re-instated, and is then tasked with going to the council races to gather allies to save Earth again through the players choices allies will be gathered, among these allies are a lot of familiar faces from previous games after this has been completed, the final battle commmences which leads to arguably one of the most controversial ending in gaming, leaving many fans upset even to this day.

Changes in the games

  1. Weapons and armour have been reworked again in the 3rd game and now depending on the gear you have equipped your power cooldown will be effected This new add-on encourages players to try new weapons and builds.
  2. Choices have a lot more weight in this game, so choices have to be considered as the ramifications could change how the game will play.
