Review of Mass Effect 3

Mass Effect 3 is the last game in the story of Commander Shepherd, released in 2012 developed by BioWare and published by EA. After the events of Mass Effect 2 Shepherd has been relieved of duty and stripped of his rank due to his time with Cerberus. He has been kept around as an "advisor" on how to deal with the Reapers. After the games start, Earth has been attacked by the Reapers.
