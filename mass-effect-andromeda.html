Mass Effect: Andromeda, is the fourth major installment in the Mass Effect series. Set 600 years after the events of the original trilogy. You play as one of the Ryder twins (Scott or Sara) who after events at the beginning of the game become the Pathfinder. The game follows the Andromeda Initiative who are trying to find new "Golden Worlds" for those from the Milky Way had somewhere to live when they were woken from cryo-sleep.
