Review of Mass Effect

Mass Effect originally was released in 2007 for the Xbox 360, then in 2008 it was ported to PC and PS3, furthermore with the release of Mass Effect: Legendary Edition the first game was remastered. It was developed by BioWare and published Microsoft Game Studios, in where the player played as Commander Shepherd as a human soldier who was tasked with investigating the re-appearance of a lost race of a highly-advanced machine race known as the Reapers

Mass Effect is a really cool game for several games!

  1. Allows players to create their own characters with their own origin stories
  2. Lots of side content, for the player to find
  3. Interesting characters with their stories that the player can help with
