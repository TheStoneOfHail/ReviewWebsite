Review of Mass Effect

Mass Effect originally was released in 2007 for the Xbox 360, then in 2008 it was ported to PC and PS3, furthermore with the release of Mass Effect: Legendary Edition the first game was remastered. It was developed by BioWare and published Microsoft Game Studios, in where the player controls Commander Shepard who is a human soldier who at the beginning of the game has been tasked with finding a beacon, which is a ancient storage device, where they see visions of an ancient race of sentient machines known as the Reapers. Shepherd is customizable to the players liking, with a number of companions that the player can recruit to help with their mission. There are plenty opportunities for exploration with many different side quests which some can have affects on the sequel games.

Mass Effect is a really cool game for several reasons!

Features the game offers

  1. Allows players to create their own characters with their own origin stories
  2. Lots of side content for the player to find
  3. Interesting characters with stories the player can help with
